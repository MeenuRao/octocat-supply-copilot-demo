# Model Comparison

## Balance Between Performance and Cost
This use-case is ideal for scenarios where you need a good trade-off between model accuracy and operational expenses. Models in this category provide reliable results for most general-purpose tasks while keeping costs manageable. Use these models for production workloads where both quality and budget are important.

## Fast, Low-Cost
This use-case is best for applications that require quick responses and minimal cost, such as high-traffic APIs, chatbots, or batch processing. Models in this category may sacrifice some accuracy or depth for speed and affordability. Choose these models when throughput and cost-efficiency are the top priorities.
